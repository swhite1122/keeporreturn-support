KeepOrReturn Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 9, 2026

Data collection

KeepOrReturn does not collect personal data.

Local storage

Deadline data is stored locally on the user's device. KeepOrReturn does not require an account and does not send deadline data to a backend service.

Analytics and advertising

KeepOrReturn does not use analytics SDKs or advertising SDKs.

Notifications

Local notifications are scheduled on device when enabled by the user. Reminder delivery depends on the user's device notification settings and system conditions.

Purchases

Purchases are handled by Apple StoreKit. Apple processes purchase transactions through its own systems.

Deleting data

Users can delete their data by deleting items in the app or by deleting the app from their device.

Contact

For help, visit KeepOrReturn Support.
