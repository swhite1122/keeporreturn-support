KeepOrReturn Support

Help for KeepOrReturn, a local-first deadline tracker for returns, trials, rentals, warranties, and other personal dates.

FAQ

How do I add a deadline?

Open KeepOrReturn, tap the add button, enter the item name and deadline details, then save.

How do reminders work?

Reminders are local notifications scheduled on your device when you enable them. Delivery depends on notification settings, focus modes, and system conditions.

How does Lifetime Unlock work?

Lifetime Unlock removes the active deadline limit with a one-time in-app purchase handled by Apple StoreKit.

How do I delete my data?

You can delete individual items in the app. You can also delete the app from your device to remove its local data.
